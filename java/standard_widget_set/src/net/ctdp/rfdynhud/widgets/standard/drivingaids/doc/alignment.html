The alignment property defines the alignment for the icons.

  • TOP_LEFT: The icons are rendered from left to right per row and from top to bottom.
  • CENTER: The icons are centered.
  • TOP_RIGHT: The icons are rendered from right to left per row and from top to bottom.
  • BOTTOM_LEFT: The icons are rendered from left to right per row and from bottom to top.
  • BOTTOM_RIGHT: The icons are rendered from right to left per row and from bottom to top.
